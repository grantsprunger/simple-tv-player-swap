Simple.TV Silverlight/HTML Player Replacer Bookmarklet

Are you having problems playing Simple.TV streams on OSX? Give this Safari bookmarklet a try.

  1. Drag the "Play in Quicktime" link to your bookmark bar in Safari.
  2. Open your Simple.TV webpage in Safari
  3. Start playing live tv or a recording
  4. Click the "Play in Quicktime" bookmark you just created
  5. Simple.TV's player is swapped out for JW Player 5 which will play the stream with Quicktime.
