Simple-tv-player-swap

Bookmarklet to swap Simple.tv Silverlight/HTML5 player for Safari Quicktime

  • Download ZIP
  • Download TAR
  • View On GitHub

This project is maintained by grantsprunger

How this works

Are you having problems playing Simple.TV streams on OSX? Give this bookmarklet a try.

  1. Drag the below Link to your bookmark bar in Safari.
  2. Open your Simple.TV webpage
  3. Start playing live tv or a recording
  4. Click your "Play in Quicktime" bookmark
  5. Simple.TV's player is swapped out for JW Player 5 which will play the stream with Quicktime.
